About Me

My name is Niña Mae Balaba, and I am a passionate graphic designer with a love for creating visually compelling stories. With over a year of experience in designing, I have honed my skills in various design disciplines, including poster making, infographic design, and digital animation.

I believe that design is more than just aesthetics; it's about communicating ideas and emotions effectively. My approach to design is rooted in understanding the vision and translating it into captivating visuals that resonate with the audience.
